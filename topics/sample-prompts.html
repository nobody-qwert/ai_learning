Sample Prompts

Effective prompt engineering is crucial for getting the best results from language models. Here are some proven prompt patterns and examples.

Chain of Thought Prompting

Let's solve this step by step: Problem: [Your problem here] Step 1: [First reasoning step] Step 2: [Second reasoning step] ... Therefore: [Final answer]

Role-Based Prompts

Example: "You are an expert software architect. Design a scalable microservices architecture for an e-commerce platform. Consider security, performance, and maintainability."

Few-Shot Learning

Provide examples to guide the model's behavior:

Example 1: Input: "Happy birthday!" → Sentiment: Positive Example 2: Input: "This is terrible." → Sentiment: Negative Example 3: Input: "The weather is okay." → Sentiment: Neutral Now analyze: "I love this new feature!" → Sentiment: ?
